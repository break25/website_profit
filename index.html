Our story

Born from a love of slow mornings.

The Brownie Loft began in a tiny Indiranagar flat with a second-hand espresso machine and a dog-eared copy of the Tartine Bread book. What started as weekend bakes shared with neighbours quietly grew into something more.

Today we are a full café, bakery, and breakfast room — but our values remain the same: unhurried preparation, honest ingredients, and the belief that breakfast deserves as much care as dinner.
